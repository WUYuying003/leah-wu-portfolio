About Me

I am a visual designer and creative storyteller with a passion for play-centered design, character creation, and narrative-driven visual experiences.

Currently completing my Master's degree at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University while interning at Tencent IEG (Tencent's gaming division), designing engagement systems and IP assets for 23,850+ active users. Previously at TikTok (ByteDance), delivering campaign visuals and mascot IP design for global live-streaming events.

I combine traditional craft with AI-augmented workflows — using Claude Code, Figma, ComfyUI, and generative AI to accelerate concept-to-delivery by ~70%. Bilingual in English and Mandarin Chinese.
